While the New Testament emphasizes grace-based giving, the tithe remains a meaningful starting point for many believers.</li><li><strong>Generous and Sacrificial:</strong> Beyond the tithe, God calls us to generous, sacrificial giving that reflects the depth of His love for us (2 Corinthians 8:1-5).</li><li><strong>Cheerful and Willing:</strong> God does not desire gifts given out of obligation or guilt. He delights in cheerful, willing hearts (2 Corinthians 9:7).</li><li><strong>Faithful and Regular:</strong> Consistent giving reflects our ongoing trust in God's provision and our commitment to the church's mission (1 Corinthians 16:2).</li></ul><h3>Why We Give</h3><p>Our giving supports the work God is doing through BCEFC:</p><ul><li>Proclaiming the Gospel in Burnaby and beyond</li><li>Discipling believers to grow in faith and maturity</li><li>Supporting missionaries and mission partners worldwide</li><li>Caring for those in need within our church and community</li><li>Maintaining a welcoming space for worship and fellowship</li></ul><p>We give not out of duty, but out of gratitude — because God first gave to us (John 3:16).</p>"},"id":"philosophy-content","isHidden":false,"type":"richtext"}]
